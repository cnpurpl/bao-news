Cat elected mayor in small town 🐱🏛️

Published on: 2023-11-15 • By: Whiskers Correspondent

In a stunning political upset, Mr. Fluffykins, a 3-year-old tabby cat, has been elected mayor of the small town of Purrington, Maine. 在一场惊人的政治颠覆中, 3岁的虎斑猫Fluffykins先生当选为缅因州Purrington镇的镇长.

The feline candidate ran on a platform of more nap times, unlimited tuna, and scratching post installations in all public buildings. 这位猫咪候选人的竞选纲领包括更多的午睡时间、无限量金枪鱼供应以及在所有公共建筑安装猫抓柱.

"He's the only candidate who truly understands the people," said local resident Sarah Johnson. "他真正了解人民的需求,"当地居民Sarah Johnson说.

Mr. Fluffykins won with 98% of the vote, with the remaining 2% going to "undecided" and a single write-in vote for a golden retriever named Max. Fluffykins先生以98%的得票率获胜, 其余2%为"未决定", 还有一张投给名叫Max的金毛犬的选票.

Political analysts suggest this marks a growing trend of anti-establishment voting in rural America. 政治分析人士认为, 这标志着美国农村地区反建制投票的趋势日益增长.

When asked for comment, Mayor Fluffykins simply licked his paw and went back to sleep. 当被要求发表评论时, Fluffykins市长只是舔了舔爪子, 然后继续睡觉.
